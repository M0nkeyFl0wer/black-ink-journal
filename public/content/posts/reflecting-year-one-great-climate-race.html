The first year of the Great Climate Race brought together runners, activists, and community members to raise awareness and funds for climate solutions. Here's a look back at the highlights and lessons learned:

Building Momentum

From the inaugural race day to community events and workshops, the Great Climate Race inspired action and fostered connections among participants.

Runners at the first Great Climate Race event

Community Impact

The event raised significant funds for renewable energy projects and brought climate action into the spotlight. Participants shared stories of personal growth and collective achievement.

Looking Ahead

As the Great Climate Race enters its second year, the focus remains on expanding its reach and impact, empowering more people to take meaningful climate action.
