How to Create New Blog Posts

  1. Log in to the admin dashboard.
  2. Click on "Create New Post".
  3. Fill in the title, content, and metadata fields.
  4. Upload or select a featured image.
  5. Choose relevant tags and set the publish date.
  6. Click "Publish" to make your post live.
